About me

Work

I currently work as a senior physiotherapist with Ospreys Rugby having spent the last four years assessing, treating and rehabilitating the senior members of the squad. This allows me to work closely with some of the worlds elite rugby players including British and Irish Lions, All Blacks, and Grand Slam champions.

Testamonials from players

Qualifications

I hold a MSc with distinction in Physiotherapy from the University of Southampton, a postgraduate certificate with distinction in Sport and Exercise Medicine from University of Wales Institute, Cardiff (UWIC), and an honours degree in Applied Psychology from University College Cork. In addition I am a qualified member of the Acupuncture Association of Chartered Physiotherapists (AACP).

Passion

My love for sport and physiotherapy and my extensive experience in both allows me to understand the nature of musculoskeletal pain especially sports injuries. I think this allows me a greater understanding of how to manage ongoing pain and dysfunction while trying to continue with your busy life and sporting commitments.
